 Chamber Music Weekend 2020

will not be held in person, thanks to the coronavirus.

 Instead . . . Join Us for a virtual HausMusik!

 every Thursday evening during June and July

It's a play-along session!

 

    Email Ellen directly to get the details about repertoire, time/date, Zoom link, and ways to support CMW and our shared mission of music-making for all.

A group enjoying String Quintet No. 3 by Dvorak

Find delight and fulfillment in playing with others in small ensembles, from duos to octets.

 

For four days we share the rare experience of recreational music-making, exploring the works of Haydn, Mendelssohn, Brahms and many others. Come and revel in the joy of creating great music in a friendly, supportive atmosphere.
